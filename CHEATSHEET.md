# BEN Cheatsheet (v1.0.0)

## Form (F)
- `F>`: Linear (Setup-Punch)
- `F?`: Interrogative (Q&A)
- `F3`: Rule of Three
- `F~`: Narrative
- `F:`: Comparative (Analogy)

## Mechanism (M)
- `M≠`: Reversal
- `M*`: Ambiguity
- `M^`: Exaggeration
- `M_`: Literalism
- `M!`: Recognition

## Status (S)
- `S↓`: Low Status
- `S↑`: High Status
- `S=`: Neutral

## Modifiers
- `(C)`: Callback
- `(T)`: Topper
- `(A)`: Act‑out

## Live Mode (Shorthand)
- Omit prefixes (`F`, `M`, `S`) if standard order is used.
- `>`: Linear (`F>`)
- `?`: Interrogative (`F?`)
- `~`: Narrative (`F~`)
- `:`: Comparative (`F:`)
- Example: `> ≠ ↓` (Linear, Reversal, Low Status)
