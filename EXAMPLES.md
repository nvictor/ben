# BEN Examples

This document serves as a workbook for BEN (Bit Engineering Notation), illustrating how to analyze and construct comedy bits using structural symbols.

## Basic Examples

### Wordplay / Pun
> "I tried a joke about chemistry — it got no reaction."

**BEN:**
```text
F> M* S=
```

### Self‑Deprecating Story
> "My father was so cheap that one year for my birthday he only gave me one walkie‑talkie..."

**BEN:**
```text
F~ M^ S↓
```
